Built for Wet Environments

Most showers fail where you can’t see.

Tile and grout are not waterproof. Traditional showers rely on materials that absorb moisture and trap it inside the system.

We build showers as complete waterproof assemblies — not surface finishes — using engineered KBRS systems and dense porcelain materials designed for constant exposure to water.

Clad vanity sinks built for bathrooms

Bathrooms destroy traditional vanities. Moisture causes swelling, staining, and failure over time.

Porcelain‑clad vanity sinks eliminate these issues by wrapping the entire surface in a non‑porous, water‑resistant material.
